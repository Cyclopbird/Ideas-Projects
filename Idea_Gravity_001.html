Idea Gravity 001

Hypothesis: Understanding Gravity Influences and Mechanisms

This idea investigates the nature of gravity, proposing influences based on particle oscillations and interactions that might explain gravitational effects.

Core Concept

Gravity might be explained or influenced by oscillatory behaviors of fundamental particles, causing force-like effects over distances. The exact mechanisms require deeper exploration and experimental validation.

Key Points

  • Proposed connection between oscillation frequencies and gravitational force strength.
  • Potential for novel interpretations of mass and inertia.
  • Encourages experiments that test oscillatory phenomena in gravitational contexts.
